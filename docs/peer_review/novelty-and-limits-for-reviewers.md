# Novelty And Limits For Reviewers

## Conservative Novelty Statement

What may be distinctive here is not any one classical ingredient by itself. The likely distinctive contribution is the finished framework that:
- separates exact projector correction, exact sector correction, and asymptotic generator correction,
- treats no-go structure as part of the core theory,
- and builds explicit operator constructions and reviewer-facing boundaries around that split.

## What Is Not Claimed As Novel
- orthogonal projection
- Knill-Laflamme conditions
- stabilizer/syndrome logic
- Helmholtz/Leray projection
- GLM divergence cleaning
- basic semigroup decay facts

## What To Treat As Modestly New Until Literature Review Deepens
- the exact sector theorem as an OCP-formalized branch rather than just a verbal QEC bridge
- the finite-time exact recovery no-go as the clean architectural separator between smooth continuous flows and exact correction
- the final architecture itself as a finished protected-state correction framework

## Limits
- the strongest theorems are still foundational and operator-theoretic rather than field-defining deep mathematics
- the QEC and continuous anchors reinterpret known structures more than they replace them
- the boundary-sensitive PDE branch remains open
- the control branch remains conditional
