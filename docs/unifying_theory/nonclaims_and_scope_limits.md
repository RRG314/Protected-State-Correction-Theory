# Nonclaims and Scope Limits

Date: 2026-04-17

1. No universal theory of all in-scope physics/dynamics.
2. No universal amount-only (rank/count/budget/history-size) exactness classifier.
3. No universal projection-preservation law across operators/domains/families.
4. No theorem-level equivalence of autonomous emergence and operator-driven correction.
5. No theorem-level promotion of two-reservoir SDS optimizer motif.

Theorem package is strong on declared ansatz/domain classes; no full global toroidal closure classification is claimed.

### SDS/tool layers
These are theorem-linked engineering systems, not independent theorem-core generators.
