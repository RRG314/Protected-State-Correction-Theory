# Quantum Feedback Into OCP

Status: what quantum mechanics clarifies inside the OCP/recoverability program after this pass.

## 1. Measurement Collapse Becomes Operational, Not Metaphorical

Quantum examples force the OCP "structure collapse" claim into explicit channel language:
- selected measurement maps preserve some target coordinates,
- and destroy or hide others through record collisions.

This sharpens OCP wording from generic "information loss" to map-specific recoverability tests.

## 2. Accessible vs Inaccessible Structure Is Target-Relative

The qubit witnesses make a clean split:
- same state family,
- same setting count,
- opposite target recoverability under basis changes (`QW001/QW002`).

This reinforces the OCP rule that recoverability is about `(family, target, record map)`, not state dimension alone.

## 3. Context Dependence Needs Decoder-Level Formalization

The context-gain family (`QW005-QW008`) gives a concrete decoder-level statement:
- each context is locally exact,
- shared decoder can still fail,
- one shared augmentation can restore invariance.

This feeds directly into the context-sensitive recoverability branch and supports keeping that package near-core.

## 4. Distributed Geometry Matters Beyond Budget Language

The two-qubit allocation split (`QW009/QW010`) clarifies OCP multi-terminal language:
- same total budget can fail if measurements are allocated to blind components,
- success requires allocation geometry aligned with target structure.

This is the quantum-side analog of multi-context anti-classifier patterns.

## 5. Decoherence Benchmarks Clarify Persistence/Fragility Terms

The dephasing sweep (`QW011-QW017`) gives a concrete fragility anchor for OCP persistence language:
- target sensitivity and exactness degrade continuously,
- complete dephasing collapses the selected target recoverability in this family.

Even though known in substance, this tightens how OCP should discuss persistence failure.

## 6. Design Guidance Gains a Safe Quantum Form

Fisher split results (`QW019-QW024`) support a narrow design rule:
- optimize for the target parameter, not measurement count or generic information surrogates.

This should remain conditional/diagnostic unless theorem closure is achieved.

## 7. What Quantum Does Not Give Back

Quantum mechanics did not justify:
- universal scalar recoverability classifiers,
- broad unification language,
- or foundational replacement claims.

## Net Feedback Result

Quantum mechanics strengthens OCP mainly by enforcing stricter target-specific language and context-aware decoder criteria. The gain is methodological and scoped-theorem packaging, not a new quantum foundation.
