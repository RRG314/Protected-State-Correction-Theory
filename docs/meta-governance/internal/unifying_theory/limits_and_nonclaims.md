# Limits and Nonclaims

Date: 2026-04-17

This section is normative scope control for the unifying-theory pass.

## 1. What Is Not Unified

1. Full nonlinear wave dynamics, MHD closure PDE dynamics, and OCP restricted-linear recoverability are not reduced to one equation class.
2. Projection-preservation behavior is not universal across domain/operator classes.
3. Emergence/self-organization dynamics are not theorem-equivalent to operator-driven correction/recoverability.
4. Two-reservoir SDS engineering effects are not promoted to theorem-core mathematical invariants.

## 2. What Remains Branch-Specific

- OCP anti-classifier package (`OCP-049`,`OCP-050`) is theorem-strong in supported restricted-linear class.
- OCP family-enlargement and mismatch fragility (`OCP-052`,`OCP-053`) are theorem-strong in declared classes.
- Soliton same-count and projection splits remain `PROVED ON SUPPORTED FAMILY` / `CONDITIONAL` / `VALIDATED` depending on claim.
- MHD annular-survivor and variable-`η` obstruction are theorem-strong on declared cylindrical/radial classes only.
- SDS Structural Discovery and Discovery Mixer are validated theorem-linked engineering systems, not universal theorem engines.

## 3. Open Gaps Blocking a Stronger General Theory

1. Continuous-theorem upgrades for soliton same-count and injectivity lanes.
2. Weighted/structured-cost anti-classifier extension beyond fixed-library unit-cost theorem.
3. Broader bounded-domain exactness classification beyond finite-mode compatible Hodge families.
4. Continuity-aware stability theorem layered directly on fiber-factorization exactness.
5. Cross-branch model-mismatch law beyond canonical restricted-linear family.

## 4. Invalid but Tempting Analogies

- “Same count failure” implies universal quantity-independence law across all branches.
- “Low divergence residual” implies bounded-domain exactness.
- “Autonomous self-organization” implies recoverability/correction equivalence.
- “SDS two-reservoir gate works in one optimizer benchmark” implies general thermodynamic theorem.

## 5. Why This Is Not a Universal Physics Theory

The surviving framework is intentionally branch-limited and object-language based. It organizes recoverability/preservation/obstruction statements where maps, families, and architecture assumptions are explicit. It does not claim a single physics law for quantum, PDE, nonlinear waves, and optimization dynamics.

## 6. Rank/Count/Budget Nonclaim (explicit)

No claim in this framework states that rank/count/budget alone determine exactness. Existing theorem package falsifies this on supported classes; any broader amount-only claim is rejected.

## 7. Self-Organization Nonclaim (explicit)

Self-organization and emergence remain separate discovery programs unless and until explicit theorem-grade transfer to recoverability/correction language is proven. Current bridge status is restricted or analogy-only.
