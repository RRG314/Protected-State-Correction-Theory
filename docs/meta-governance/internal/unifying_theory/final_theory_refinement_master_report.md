## 1. Purpose and Scope

This pass refines the existing branch-limited framework centered on **Structure-Dependent Recoverability and Failure Theory**. It does **not** introduce new branches, does **not** promote analogy-only material, and does **not** assert a universal theory of everything.

The refinement objective was to make the framework:
1. cleaner,
2. tighter,
3. more rigorous,
4. more internally consistent,
5. harder to attack under skeptical review,
6. clearly stratified into universal core vs branch-limited packages.

## 2. Baseline Preserved

The baseline retained and sharpened:
- Universal core: exact recoverability as factorization/fiber-constancy on a declared admissible family.
- Universal no-go: exact recovery fails under target-distinguishing collisions.
- Branch-limited strengthening: anti-classifier package, family-enlargement fragility, model-mismatch instability, domain/topology obstruction, symmetry-quotient obstruction.

Not promoted:
- universal amount-only law,
- universal projection-preservation law,
- universal emergence/correction equivalence,
- SDS/two-reservoir motif as theorem-core universal law.
