## Positioning Policy

Each branch claim is tagged as one of:
- likely standard,
- standard but repackaged usefully,
- plausible restricted extension,
- plausibly distinct,
- too weak to matter.

## What Could Become Distinct

Most plausible distinct lane (if upgraded):
1. A continuous restricted injectivity theorem on the declared quotient manifold for one explicit observation class, plus
2. a matched continuous noninjectivity theorem for an equal-count observation class, plus
3. explicit no-noise ambiguity floor lower bound with constants on that pair.

That theorem/no-go pair would go beyond current finite-grid validation and support stronger branch admission.

## Current Position Statement
