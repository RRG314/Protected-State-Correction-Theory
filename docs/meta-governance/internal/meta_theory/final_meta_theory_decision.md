# Final Meta-Theory Decision

Date: 2026-04-17
Decision pass: falsification-first

## Decision

**Outcome C: Promote a branch-limited meta-theory of structure-dependent interaction and recoverability/failure.**

Not promoted:
- universal “everything has two parts” law,
- universal physics unification claims,
- one-equation impossibility claims.

## Surviving Meta-Theory (Formal)

Let `S = (A, T, M, C, E, D, ~)` with declared admissible family `A`.

Exact recoverability/correction in the tested ecosystem is controlled by compatibility between target role `T` and one or more interacting structural roles (`M`, `C`, domain/symmetry/evolution constraints). Failure arises when target-relevant distinctions are not preserved under that structure (collisions, hidden kernel directions, symmetry blindness, model/domain mismatch).

This is a **branch-limited organizing theory**:
- universal core: factorization/fiber-collision theorems,
- stronger laws: branch-specific theorem packages.

## Surviving Meta-Theory (Plain Language)

A single amount number is usually not enough. What matters is whether the system’s observation/correction/representation structure is compatible with what you want to preserve.
