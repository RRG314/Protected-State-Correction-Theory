# Physics Translation Theorem Boundary

Date: 2026-04-19

## Purpose

Separate physics-linked statements into:
- theorem-grade mapping,
- validated branch-limited mapping,
- analogy/context only.

## Required nonclaims in physics-facing text

1. Do not claim paradox resolution.
2. Do not claim gravity is made of information.
3. Do not claim universal scalar invariants.
4. If a statement is from surrogate channels, say so directly in the statement.
