Fibers:
- fields that produce the same coarse record, truncated modal data, divergence data, or other observation map

Target:
- divergence-free field, selected modal functional, or bounded-domain protected quantity

Exact recovery:
- the target is constant on the PDE-side record fibers of the supported family

Impossibility:
- divergence-only or under-resolved records leave target-mixed fibers

Best kept examples:
- periodic projection exactness
- bounded-domain transplant failure
- divergence-only bounded no-go
- periodic modal threshold laws as fiber-threshold laws on the tested family

Strongest exact match:
- coarse-record ambiguity and exact/no-go threshold language on restricted PDE-side families

Strongest limit:
- no general nonlinear PDE fiber geometry is claimed here beyond the supported families.
