# Control / Observability Through Fiber Language

Fibers:
- states indistinguishable under the current observation history

Target:
- full state, chosen coordinate, or protected functional

Exact recovery:
- the target is constant on the observation-history fibers

Asymptotic recovery:
- finite-history fibers remain target-mixed, but observer dynamics eventually separate the target over time

Branch anchor:
- the diagonal/history threshold laws and observer split are the main kept examples here

Strongest kept example:
- one-step exact failure / two-step exact success / observer asymptotic success on the control toy family

Strongest limit:
- full classical detectability is broader than the branch's target-relative coarsening notion.
