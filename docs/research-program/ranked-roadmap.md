# Ranked Contribution Roadmap

## Ranking Criteria

Each branch is ranked on:
- novelty potential
- usefulness potential
- theorem potential
- computational potential
- risk of collapsing into standard material
- risk of wasting time

Scores are qualitative and intentionally conservative.

## Top 3 Branches To Push Hard

### 1. Constrained-observation recoverability
Why:
- strongest current theorem potential
- strongest open threshold and lower-bound direction
- already has clean falsification discipline and real computational support

### 2. Restricted-linear / design-engine layer
Why:
- strongest practical/useful branch
- now has a finished minimal-augmentation theorem
- naturally produces templates, diagnostics, and measurable design outputs

### 3. Exact continuous / CFD bounded-domain lane
Why:
- the bounded-domain problem is mathematically serious
- it now has both a real negative result and a real positive restricted theorem
- the next step is clear rather than vague
