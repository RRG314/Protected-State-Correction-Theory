# Lens Promotion Decisions (2026-04-16)

## Decision Standard

Lens-derived content is promoted only if it does at least one of:
- sharpens an exactness criterion,
- sharpens a no-go or obstruction,
- provides a computable invariant with predictive value beyond rank/count/budget,
- or creates attackable open-problem structure with clear falsifiers.

Pure rewording is not promoted.

## What Was Promoted Now

### 1. Operator-theory payload (primary)

Promoted:
- alignment/kernel invariant language (`ker(T*)`, row-space inclusion, alignment score) as the primary explanation of same-rank opposite exactness verdicts in constrained-observation and fiber branches.
- spectral-abscissa / gap rate language in asymptotic branches where split-preserving generator assumptions hold.

Promotion type:
- theorem-spine interpretation sharpening, not theorem-ID replacement.

Why promoted:
- improves predictive and diagnostic power over rank-only wording,
- directly matches existing proved claims (`OCP-031`, `OCP-043`, `OCP-046`, `OCP-047`, `OCP-049`, `OCP-050`, `OCP-052`, `OCP-053`).

### 2. Functional-analysis payload (primary for continuous lanes)

Promoted:
- bounded-domain/Hodge/topological obstruction framing as the principal bounded-domain gate,
- semigroup/spectral rate formulations for asymptotic branches.

Promotion type:
- no-go and exactness classification sharpening,
- open-problem targeting toward boundary/topology-sensitive exactness criteria.

Why promoted:
- strongest route for bounded-domain theorem expansion,
- directly explains why periodic transplants fail without boundary-compatible structure.

### 3. Geometry payload (supporting layer only)

Promoted narrowly:
- principal-angle/alignment geometry where it corresponds to explicit operator facts already proved.

Not promoted as foundation:
- universal geometry language,
- branch flattening into one quotient story.

Why:
- useful when theorem-grade and computable, redundant otherwise.

## What Was Kept But Not Promoted

### Investigation-only / secondary

- IT fractional recoverability (`FR`) retained only as optional ranking metric in constrained-observation discussion.
- DS transition framing retained only as a secondary asymptotic interpretation.

Reason:
- both fail to strengthen exact branches or branch theorem spine.

## What Was Explicitly Rejected

Rejected from promotion:
- inverse-problem framing on exact projector / exact QEC / exact Helmholtz anchors,
- entropy wording for exactness statements,
- one-lens universalization,
- dynamical-language overlays on one-shot exact branches.

Reason:
- these are either weaker than current theorem language or actively misleading.

## Consequence for Theory Formation

Promotion decisions support one branch-limited theory shape:
- a restricted recoverability-structure theory centered on alignment/factorization/collision-gap/augmentation in constrained-observation and fiber-limit branches,
- plus boundary/topology obstruction law in bounded-domain branches.

They do **not** support a universal one-language theory claim across all branches.
