# Novelty And Limits

## What Is Not Claimed As New

These are foundational but not novelty claims:
- orthogonal projection in Hilbert spaces,
- Knill-Laflamme exact correctability,
- stabilizer syndrome logic,
- Helmholtz/Leray projection,
- Dedner-style GLM cleaning,
- standard linear observer or separation-principle background.

OCP should not pretend to have discovered those ingredients.

## What May Be Distinctive Here

The credible distinctive layer is narrower:
- organizing exact projector recovery, QEC syndrome recovery, and divergence cleaning under one protected-state correction language,
- separating exact correction from asymptotic correction in a way that remains operator-level rather than rhetorical,
- promoting indistinguishability and insufficient correction structure as first-class no-go criteria,
- and turning this into explicit design rules rather than a broad analogy memo.

## Where The Repo Is Strongest

1. It has a clean exact finite-dimensional theorem.
2. It has a clean exact continuous anchor through Helmholtz projection.
3. It has a real impossibility result.
4. It avoids promoting weak ML/optimizer bridges.

## Where The Repo Is Not Yet Strong Enough

1. It does not yet contain a paper-level theorem that experts would immediately see as new outside the reinterpretive framework.
2. It does not yet settle a universal correction-capacity concept.
3. It does not yet turn the control extension into a strong theorem rather than a careful conditional branch.

## Failure Modes This Repo Explicitly Avoids

- treating every control law as OCP just because it has feedback,
- treating every correction term as orthogonal by assertion,
- treating QEC language as automatically meaningful in PDE settings,
- treating Topological Adam or optimizer heuristics as if they were exact correction theory,
- and confusing useful conceptual unification with novelty at theorem level.

## Honest Bottom Line

The strongest honest positioning today is:
- **GOOD**, not excellent.
- The framework is real enough to guide design and proof work.
- The novelty is in the formal bridge and falsification-first architecture, not yet in a major new universal theorem.
